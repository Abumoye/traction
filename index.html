Common Questions

What does Traction Outsourcing do?

Traction Outsourcing Limited is a management consulting and HR solutions firm based in Abuja, Nigeria, serving clients across all major industries throughout Africa. Services include talent acquisition, HR process outsourcing, learning and development, startup structuring, corporate restructuring, business strategy consulting, and corporate retreats. ATH Recruiters, the recruitment arm of the firm, connects businesses with vetted, qualified talent across sectors.

Where is Traction Outsourcing located in Abuja?

Traction Outsourcing Limited is based at 26, Algiers Street, Wuse Zone 5, FCT, Abuja.

Is Traction Outsourcing a management consulting firm in Nigeria?

Yes. Traction Outsourcing operates as a management consulting and business advisory firm serving companies across Nigeria, with a particular focus on staffing, HR, structuring, and governance work for growing businesses and SMEs.
