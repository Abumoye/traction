Traction Outsourcing Limited ("Traction Outsourcing Limited," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through tolnigeria.com, why we collect it, and how we handle it. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways.

Information you give us directly. When you fill in a consultation form on this site, we collect your full name, phone number, company name, and email address. If you contact us through WhatsApp, phone, or email instead, we receive whatever information you choose to share in that conversation.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our hosting provider may log basic technical information such as your browser type and general access times, for security and maintenance purposes. This site does not currently run analytics or advertising tracking scripts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your consultation request and get in touch about our services.
  • To understand which of our service pages generate enquiries, so we can improve them.
  • To send you information you have specifically asked for, such as event details or proposals.

We do not use your information for automated decision-making, and we do not sell your information to third parties.

3. Where Your Information Is Stored

Consultation form submissions are stored in a Google Sheet within a private Google Drive folder, accessible only to authorized staff of Traction Outsourcing Limited. Google's own privacy and security practices apply to how that infrastructure is secured. We do not maintain a separate customer database beyond this.

4. When We Share Information

We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers who help us operate (such as Google, for form storage), or where required to do so by Nigerian law or a valid legal process.

5. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels. If that changes in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect it, and where required, you will be asked for consent.

6. Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to know what information we hold about you, to request a correction if it is inaccurate, and to request that we delete it, subject to any legal or contractual obligations that require us to keep it. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Data Retention

We keep consultation form submissions for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your enquiry and maintain business records, or until you ask us to delete it, whichever comes first.

8. Children's Privacy

This website and our services are directed at businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

9. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our services or applicable law change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
