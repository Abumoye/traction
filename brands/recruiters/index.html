What Is ATH Recruiters?

ATH Recruiters, also widely known as Abuja Talent Hub, is the official staffing brand of Traction Outsourcing Limited and one of the recruitment agencies in Abuja serving businesses across Nigeria. This is the official website for both ATH Recruiters and Abuja Talent Hub. We were built to solve one specific problem: getting Nigerian businesses and homes the right person, fast, and affordably. We maintain a verified community of ready-to-work candidates, from entry-level graduates to experienced professionals, so that when you need staff, you are not starting from zero, you are choosing from people who are already screened and available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ATH Recruiters the same as Abuja Talent Hub?

Yes. ATH Recruiters is the official recruitment brand also known as Abuja Talent Hub, operated by Traction Outsourcing Limited. This is the official website for both ATH Recruiters and Abuja Talent Hub, and both names refer to the same verified community of ready-to-work candidates.

Is ATH Recruiters an affordable recruitment agency in Nigeria?

Yes. ATH Recruiters is built to be one of the more affordable recruitment agencies in Nigeria, with plans starting from ₦30,000 for select roles, keeping pricing accessible for small businesses and private individuals, not just large corporations.

Does ATH Recruiters handle mass recruitment in Nigeria and Abuja?

Yes. Plan A is built for mass recruitment and high-volume hiring, with unlimited candidate requests during your subscription and a 24 to 48 hour turnaround once onboarded, suited to businesses in Abuja and across Nigeria that need to fill many roles quickly.

Does ATH Recruiters offer graduate recruitment and training in Nigeria?

Yes. ATH Recruiters sources and places graduate talent for entry-level and junior roles, and Plan C includes structured onboarding and skills training delivered by our management consultants, making it a practical option for businesses looking to train graduates in Nigeria or Abuja.

Is ATH Recruiters a domestic staff recruitment agency in Nigeria?

Yes. Alongside businesses, ATH Recruiters works as a domestic staff recruitment agency in Abuja and across Nigeria, placing housekeepers, cooks, drivers, and other personal staff for private homes and individuals who need people they can trust.

Which cities and states does ATH Recruiters operate in?

ATH Recruiters is headquartered in Abuja and places candidates in all states across Nigeria, covering businesses, hotels, restaurants, shops, and private homes nationwide.

How fast can ATH Recruiters fill a role?

Turnaround depends on the plan. Plan A offers a 24 to 48 hour turnaround for subscribers, Plan B typically delivers qualified candidates within 2 weeks for specialized roles, and Plan C is a fully managed process suited to leadership and bulk hires.
