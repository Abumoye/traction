Prince Elijah Abumoye

Visionary Entrepreneur & Senior Corporate Executive

Prince Elijah Abumoye, born September 5th, 1994, is the visionary entrepreneur and founder of Traction Outsourcing Limited. He is a seasoned business leader, author, corporate trainer, and senior executive with extensive hands-on leadership experience, including past executive roles as a Chief Operating Officer (COO) and Country Head of HR for a multinational organization working across Nigeria, Rwanda, and Dubai. Through Traction Outsourcing, Traction Properties, and ATH Recruiters, he has worked with several businesses across Nigeria.

Prince Elijah holds a BSc from the University of Lagos. With a strong foundation in corporate governance, workforce collaboration, and strategic human resource development, Prince Elijah leverages his rich background to help businesses establish structures and high-performing teams across Nigeria.

"Deeply committed to an Africa that works for Africans."
