Traction International Corporate Retreat, 2026

Date: November 21st - 28th, 2026 | Location: Kigali, Rwanda

Prepare Your Business for 2027

The Traction International Corporate Retreat is a premier yearly gathering crafted explicitly for CEOs, senior management executives, and ambitious entrepreneurs who take building institutional business structures seriously. This dual-purpose executive event is designed to step completely out of routine operational environments, bringing visionary leaders into close contact with billionaire mentors while exploring Africa's fastest-growing business hubs.

Over the course of this highly immersive trip, you will participate in masterclasses built to establish structural clarity, optimize workforce management strategies, and secure cross-border insights to solidify your enterprise layout ahead of the 2027 financial year.

  • Elite Business Session: 5-day intensive fireside chat and vision-building sessions with industry giants.
  • Curated Exploration: 2-day all-expense-paid strategic tour to historical and tourism sites across Kigali.
