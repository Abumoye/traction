Business Advisory

The decisions that move a business forward rarely have a clean answer. We sit on your side of the table and help you make the call with the full picture in front of you.

A joint venture term sheet. A vesting structure that needs to survive twenty years. A dormant contract that needs to either be revived or ended cleanly. These are not situations with a textbook answer, they are situations where the details of your specific business decide the outcome. We advise on the actual deal in front of you, not a generic framework.

From The Case Files

A look at how advisory engagements actually run, drawn from real work.

Hospitality Venue — Nightclub Joint Venture

Negotiation

A lounge inside an established hotel, being reimagined as a nightclub under a new operator. We prepared the full negotiation position, opening terms through to walk-away terms, across eight deal points, and identified bar revenue and beverage control as the highest-margin point worth holding firm on. The engagement closed with a signed Memorandum of Understanding under Nigerian law, with arbitration seated in Abuja.

Family-Owned Holding Company — Long-Term Vesting Structure

Structuring

A vesting agreement built to hold for twenty years needs to be right the first time. We structured a dual-tranche vesting arrangement, split across years one to five and years six to ten, with claims only exercisable at the five and ten-year milestones, and a twenty-year sale restriction from incorporation to protect the structure long after the advisory engagement ends.

Restaurant Business — Dormant Contract Review

Contract Strategy

Not every advisory question is about growth. Sometimes it is about deciding whether a stalled agreement is worth reviving. We reviewed the dormancy, recommended an activation-then-terminate strategy, and drafted the formal activation letter with a fourteen-business-day response deadline tied to the relevant contract clause.
