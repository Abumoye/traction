Startup Structuring

Every strong business is standing on a structure nobody sees. We build that structure first, so the business you are growing does not outgrow itself.

Most Nigerian businesses do not fail because the idea was weak. They fail because the founder was still writing job descriptions from memory in month eighteen, still deciding pay by conversation, still the only person who knew how anything worked. Growth without structure just means more people relying on a system that was never actually built. We fix that before it becomes expensive.

How We Build The Foundation

Three stages, done in sequence, so nothing is designed on top of a gap.

1

Registration & Preliminaries

We help you set the tone of your business nomenclature, ensuring that it is unique and distinguishable. At this phase, we register your business or company (CAC registration), get you a Nigeria Revenue Service (NRS) Tax Identification Number, create a brand image for you, trademark your name in the appropriate class, get you your VAT certificate, create your official communication letters according to Corporate Affairs Commission stipulations and the Company and Allied Matters Act (CAMA), and register your company with three top-tier banks in Nigeria. Ensuring that you have a solid head start.

2

Design and Document

We create a functional and capital organogram with reporting lines and role architecture around where the business is going, not just where it is today. We create job descriptions, KPIs, staff policy handbooks, and onboarding material. This gives you a concrete foundation to build your legacy business from day zero.

3

Deploy

We train, assign roles, and ensure your team understands the fine print. We hand over a structure the team can step into immediately, with training where it is needed, so the business is not relying on the founder to explain it every time someone new joins.

Common Questions

What does my startup need to get started properly?

Beyond CAC registration, a new business needs a clear organogram and reporting lines, job descriptions and KPIs, a staff policy handbook, and onboarding material, the structure that lets the business run without the founder explaining everything from scratch every time someone new joins.

What is startup structuring?

Startup structuring is the process of building the legal, organizational, and operational foundation of a new business, registration and preliminaries, an organogram and governance framework, and the documentation the business needs from day one, rather than adding structure after problems appear.
