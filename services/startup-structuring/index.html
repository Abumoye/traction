Startup Structuring

Every strong business is standing on a structure nobody sees. We build that structure first, so the business you are growing does not outgrow itself.

Most Nigerian businesses do not fail because the idea was weak. They fail because the founder was still writing job descriptions from memory in month eighteen, still deciding pay by conversation, still the only person who knew how anything worked. Growth without structure just means more people relying on a system that was never actually built. We fix that before it becomes expensive.

How We Build The Foundation

Four stages, done in sequence, so nothing is designed on top of a gap.

1

Diagnose

We sit inside the business first. Not a questionnaire, an actual look at how decisions get made, where the bottlenecks sit, and what the founder is still doing that should belong to someone else.

2

Design

We design the organogram, the reporting lines, and the role architecture around where the business is going, not just where it is today. Structure built for the current headcount alone is structure you will outgrow in a year.

3

Document

Job descriptions, KPIs, staff policy handbooks, onboarding material. If it only lives in the founder's head, it is not a structure yet. We put it on paper in a form the business can actually run on.

4

Deploy

We hand over a structure the team can step into immediately, with training where it is needed, so the business is not relying on the founder to explain it every time someone new joins.

What This Looks Like In Practice

Retail & Personal Care Business

Gifting & Retail Business
