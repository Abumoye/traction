Where We Deliver

In Nigeria

Physical classes run in Abuja, Lagos, Enugu, Anambra, Ibadan, Asaba, Kaduna, and Kano. A minimum of 5 participants is required for a physical class within Nigeria.

Beyond Nigeria

Physical classes are also available in Kigali (Rwanda), Accra (Ghana), and Nairobi (Kenya), with a minimum of 15 participants for physical classes outside Nigeria.

Online

Every course in the catalogue is also available online, with no minimum number of participants, so a single employee or a full team can enrol either way.

Common Questions

What training courses does Traction Outsourcing offer?

The catalogue covers five tracks: Leadership & Management Development, Business Finance & Accounting, AI & Computer Literacy, Workplace Management, and Customer Service & Client Experience, alongside bespoke training built around a specific gap on your team.

Are the courses certified?

Yes. All courses in the catalogue are certificate courses, issued on completion.

Can training be done online, or does it have to be in person?

Both. Every course in the catalogue is available online with no minimum number of participants, and most are also available as physical classes in select cities.

Do you offer training outside Nigeria?

Yes. Physical classes are available in Kigali, Accra, and Nairobi in addition to eight Nigerian cities, and every course is available online anywhere.

How much do the courses cost?

Fees vary by course, delivery mode, and group size. Reach out to us with the course you are interested in and we will confirm current pricing for it.

How do I train my staff in Abuja or across Nigeria?

Choose a course from the catalogue above, or tell us the gap on your team if it is not listed, and reach out through the form or WhatsApp on this page. We deliver both in Abuja and across Nigeria, online or in person.

Can Traction Outsourcing train a large group, up to 1,000 employees, in Abuja?

Yes. Group size scales with the engagement, from a handful of employees to large corporate rollouts of hundreds or more, delivered online, in person, or a mix of both depending on what the group needs.

What is the minimum number of participants for a physical class?

Five participants for physical classes within Nigeria, and fifteen for physical classes outside Nigeria. Online classes have no minimum.
