Staff Outsourcing & HR Management

We manage the people side of your business, from sourcing and payroll to compliance and HR administration, so you can focus on running the business, not the paperwork.

Every growing business reaches a point where managing people becomes its own full-time job. Payroll has to go out correctly and on time. Pension and tax remittances have to be filed without error. New hires need onboarding, exiting staff need proper offboarding, and somewhere in between, someone has to keep track of attendance, performance, and the paperwork that protects the business if anything goes wrong. Most founders did not start their business to manage all of that, and most internal teams are not built to carry it at scale.

Traction Outsourcing takes on the staffing and HR administration work directly, so your outsourced staff show up, perform, and get paid correctly, while the compliance and management weight sits with us instead of you. This is not a call center reading from a script. We look at how your business actually operates before we place a single person, then build the staffing and HR support around that reality.

Common Questions

What is staff outsourcing?

Staff outsourcing is when a business hands over the sourcing, payroll, compliance, and day-to-day management of a set of employees to a specialist firm, while those staff continue working within the client's business. It removes the administrative and legal weight of employment without removing the workforce itself.

How does staff outsourcing work in Nigeria?

An outsourcing partner like Traction Outsourcing sources and places staff, manages payroll and statutory remittances such as PAYE, pension, and NHF, handles onboarding and exit, and takes on the HR administration and compliance work that would otherwise sit with the client's internal team.

What is the difference between staff outsourcing and recruitment?

Recruitment ends once a candidate is placed and the client takes over as employer. Staff outsourcing continues after placement, the outsourcing firm remains responsible for payroll, compliance, HR administration, and ongoing management of that staff member for as long as the arrangement runs.

Does Traction Outsourcing handle payroll and pension remittance?

Yes. Payroll administration, including PAYE, pension, and NHF remittances, is a core part of the staff outsourcing service, run to Nigerian statutory requirements.

Can Traction Outsourcing manage staff outside Abuja?

Yes. Traction Outsourcing manages outsourced staff for businesses across Nigeria, not only in Abuja, with payroll, compliance, and HR administration handled remotely and consistently regardless of where the staff are based.
